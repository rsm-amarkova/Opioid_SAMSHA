Statistical Story

Suggested structure — you can overwrite all this text.

1. Research Question

We examine whether proximity to SAMHSA-affiliated clinics mitigates the impact of low income on opioid overdose rates at the county level.

2. Key Variables

  • Outcome: Overdose rate per 100,000 residents.
  • Drivers: Median household income, % uninsured, unemployment rate.
  • Access: Distance to nearest SAMHSA clinic or clinics per 100k.

3. Model Outline

We estimate a linear regression with interaction terms to test whether the effect of socioeconomic status varies by treatment access:

od_rate = β₀ + β₁·income + β₂·access + β₃·(income × access) + controls + ε
